Enrol someone in a single credit-bearing module — days, not months. No need to commit anyone to a full qualification up front.

Every module carries credits toward the Occupational Certificate: Computer Technician (NQF 5, 282 credits). Learners build toward certification at their own pace, around their work.

Skills spend that scores

Delivered through an accredited provider, so each module supports your B-BBEE Skills Development scorecard — recognised training, properly documented.

A qualification that's theirs to keep

Employees earn a national, portable credential — a real reason to stay, learn, and grow with SPS.
