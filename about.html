Put your people in the driver's seat

A path they own

AI is changing how the energy sector works — and the people who get ahead of it will be the ones who shape where it goes. Through Centenary Networks, your people choose their modules, build credits on their own schedule, and ladder toward a national qualification as far as they want to take it. Not training handed down — a path they own.

  • Short, credit-bearing modules that fit around the job
  • Credits that accumulate toward the Occupational Certificate: Computer Technician
  • A national, portable credential that's theirs to keep
  • Skills spend that's documented and supports your B-BBEE scorecard
